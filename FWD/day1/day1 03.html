Recipe 2

Chicken biryani

Ingredients:

  • Coriander Leaves
  • Cilantro
  • Dhaniya
  • dhania, Kothamalli ilai, Sambhar, Kothimbir, Kothmir
  • chilly powder
  • oil
  • salt
  • rice
  • Chicken, Onions, tomatoes, turmeric, and garam masala Saffron and fried onions for garnish

Method of Preparation:

Step 1:Rice preparation: Wash and soak the basmati rice for about 30 minutes. Parboil the rice until it's about 70% cooked and set it aside.

step 2:Sautéing: In a large pot, sauté sliced onions until golden brown, add tomatoes, and spices.

step 3 Layering: In the same pot, layer the partially cooked rice over the meat/vegetable mixture. Sprinkle saffron milk, fried onions, and a bit of garam masala between the layers.

step 4 Dum Cooking: Cover the pot with a tight lid or seal it with dough to trap the steam (known as "dum" cooking). Cook on low heat for about 20-30 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and infused with flavors.

step 5

Serve: Gently mix the layers before serving, garnish with fresh coriander leaves, fried onions, and serve hot with raita (yogurt sauce) or salad.
